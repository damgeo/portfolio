A Final Word

If you’ve completed all three scenarios, you should now be ready to help develop a culture of respect and an environment that is inclusive for all by:

  • Treating others with respect.
  • Recognising and challenging inappropriate behaviour.
  • Identifying opportunities for inclusivity.

Activity for Acting Differently
You may need a paper and pen to note down your thoughts for this activity.
Think about the people in your social circles at university, for example, your housemates, classmates, club or society friends, and the occasions when you meet up with one another. Are there times when you would like to feel more welcome and included in the group and to have your needs and views heard? Consider how you might assert your needs and views within the group to feel more included. How might you make other people feel more welcome and included and ensure their needs and views are heard? Is there anyone in particular you can think of that may require your help?
